Welcome to Mysuru Royal Institute of Technology (MRIT)

MRIT is an esteemed engineering college committed to providing world-class education and fostering innovation. Located in the heritage city of Mysuru, Karnataka, MRIT aims to shape the next generation of engineers, leaders, and innovators who will contribute to society's progress.

Our mission is to provide high-quality education while ensuring holistic development. We strive to instill strong values, creativity, and ethics to guide our students to positively impact the world.

Academic Programs

MRIT offers a wide variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to equip students with knowledge and skills for their careers. Some of our core programs include:

  • Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Computer Science Engineering
  • Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Civil Engineering
  • Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Electronics and communication Engineering
  • Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Mechanical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Information Science and Engineering
  • Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in AIML Engineering

Additionally, we offer postgraduate programs for those looking to deepen their expertise.
